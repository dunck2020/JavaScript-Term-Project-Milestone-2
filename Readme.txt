Thank you for viewing my game website!  This site is dedicaed to all of those that
love the classic games.  Pong and snake have been around as long as I can remember.
Feel free to come and visit as often as you like.

Speed snake is a game where you control a snake and must avoid contact with 
the walls and with the snake body.  Collect to grow and increase the speed.

Landscape puzzle is for the puzzle lovers, choose your background and have fun
with the puzzle.

Pong is a great game that you need a partner to play with.   Challenge your
friend and increase the speed as you aster the game.
